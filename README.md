    _ SECTION I. OF HUME'S PHILOSOPHICAL CONSISTENCY.

    _ SECTION II. OF RELIGIOUS HYPOCRISY.

    _ SECTION III. ON THE NATURAL DIGNITY OF THE LITERARY CHARACTER, AND THE REASONS WHICH HAVE BROUGHT IT INTO CONTEMPT.
